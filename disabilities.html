Types of disabilities

Dyslexia

Dyslexia is the most well-known reading disorder. It specifically impairs a person's ability to read. The immersive reader helps overcoming this disability

Tremors

this may be genetic or non - genetic. It causes shaking of hands that prevent the person from using devices such as the keyboard and mouse. The sticky keys help in overcoming this disability

Audio Impairment

An audio impairment is a person's reduced ability to hear sounds. He may not be able to interpret sounds properly and may hear less in either or both ears. There is headphone volume adjustment along with special audio string available in order to overcome this disability

Glaucoma

It is a disability where the person is unable to read small texts and requires the use of a magnifier in order to read it. The magnifier tool helps in overcoming this disability.
