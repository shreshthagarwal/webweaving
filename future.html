Going into the future

As we go into the future technology advancements are beyond imagination. Virtual reality, advanced automobiles and improved devices such as mobile phones, laptops, simulators have allowed people to progress in different fields

Virtual reality (VR)

Virtual Reality (VR) is a computer-generated environment with scenes and objects that appear to be real, making the user feel they are immersed in their surroundings. This environment is perceived through a device known as a Virtual Reality headset or helmet. Before people had to travel halfway across the globe in order to see a monument per say. With the introduction of television and smartphones people could see images of these places with a click. Now however, we have achieved virtual reality. It allows the user to live in that place without having to leave the room. It is not only implemented in gaming but also is used for military training and sightseeing

Automobiles

Companies such as Tesla, Morris Garages have introduced cars that are enabled with the highest level of technology. Features such as auto - parking, CAR CALL feature, self driving modes, audio interactions through Alexa have allowed people to use automobiles in the easiest manner ever. These advancements have revolutionised the world we live in today.
