What is Immersive Technology

Immersive Technology is an integration of virtual content with the physical environment in a way that allows the users to engage naturally with the blended reality.

What is Immersive Reader

Immersive Reader is a full screen reading experience to increase readability of Content Learning Tools that are designed to support students with dyslexia and dysgraphia in the classroom, but can support anyone who wants to make reading on their device easier. It is available on most platforms such as Microsoft applications

What is Magnifier

Magnifier is a useful tool that enlarges part-or all-of your screen so you can see the words and images better. It comes with a few different settings, so you can use it the way that suits you best. You can open and close Magnifier quickly so it's handy when you need it and it's out of your way when you don't.

What is Sticky Keys

Sticky Keys is a feature in Windows that allows modifier keys like Ctrl and Shift to remain active even after when you're not pressing them. This can help users with physical impairments, who have trouble pressing two keys at a time (like using Shift to make uppercase letters)
